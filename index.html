Must-see attraction sites in Pietermaritzburg

Pietermaritzburg presents a multitude of compelling attractions, ranging from historical landmarks and cultural engagements to outdoor pursuits. One of the prominent points of interest in Pietermaritzburg is the renowned Pietermaritzburg City Hall, distinguished for its impressive colonial architecture and rich historical significance.

Additionally, the Tatham Art Gallery stands out as a noteworthy destination, boasting a diverse array of artworks from both local and international artists. Nature enthusiasts can find solace in the tranquillity of the KwaZulu-Natal National Botanical Garden, which features a plethora of indigenous flora and scenic walking paths.
